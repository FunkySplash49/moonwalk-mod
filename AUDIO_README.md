# Michael Jackson Sound Files

This mod plays one of 5 random .ogg files when the player presses M.

You must provide your own audio files. Place exactly 5 OGG Vorbis files in BOTH:

  NeoForge:  src/main/resources/assets/moonwalkmod/sounds/mj1.ogg ... mj5.ogg
  Fabric:    src/main/resources/assets/moonwalkmod/sounds/mj1.ogg ... mj5.ogg

Requirements:
- Format: OGG Vorbis (.ogg). Use Audacity > Export As OGG.
- Mono or stereo, 44.1 kHz recommended.
- Keep clips short (a few seconds) to avoid long playback.

NOTE ON COPYRIGHT:
Michael Jackson recordings are copyrighted. Only distribute the mod with audio
you have the right to use. For personal/private use you may use clips you own legally.

The sound IDs are registered as: moonwalkmod:mj1 ... moonwalkmod:mj5
and referenced in assets/moonwalkmod/sounds.json.
